FAQ

Is my data private?

Yes. NextNote stores your notes securely on your device and syncs via iCloud only if enabled.

Does it work offline?

Yes. You can create and edit notes offline; sync resumes when you're back online.

What devices are supported?

NextNote works on iPhone and iPad, with a macOS version coming soon.

Do I need an account?

No account required. Just install the app and start writing.
